Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  3. Transfer the churned ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.
